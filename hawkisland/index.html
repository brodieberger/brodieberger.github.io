Hawk Island Marina

ONLY 2 MILES NORTH OF PHILADELPHIA, PA & CAMDEN, NJ

Hawk Island Marina is conveniently located at the mouth of the RANCOCAS CREEK. Offering quick and easy access to both the Tacony and Burlington Bristol Bridges.

In October 2003, John and Karen purchased Hawk Island Marina. Both with family roots in Delanco, NJ and long-time patrons of this marina, they knew that this would be a great opportunity to expand upon the existing marina and incorporate sound environmental policies while maintaining the ecosystem and providing a safe, fun, and positive recreational experience.
